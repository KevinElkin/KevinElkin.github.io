Data Sources and Preprocessing

Brief Biography

Zachary David Alexander Efron was born on October 18, 1987, in San Luis Obispo, California. At age 11, one of Zac's piano teachers recognized Efron's talents and persuaded him to pursue theater and acting at the Pacific Conservatory of the Performing Arts. Zac auditioned for a small part in the production of Gypsy which marked the beginning of his passion for acting. Soon, Efron was making regular trips to Los Angeles for auditions while attending Arroyo Grande High School. Efron's first major role landed him a spot in the hit television series Fireflies which begin his Hollywood acting carrier

Bubble Chart

Below is a packed bubble chart categorizing the various generes and movies Zac Efron has taken part in. Each large (or parent) bubble represents a different genre in film and the small bubbles inside represent a film that Zac Efron has taken part in. Scrolling over each film (or smaller bubble) reveals their IMDb score as well as the name of the film. Additionally, I chose to automatically show the titles of the movies with an IMDb score over 6.5. This was advantageous for the visualization as it allowed me to show which generes of Zac Efron's movies are the most popular as well as showcase the most popular films he's in of each category (Showing the most popular movies with a score > 6.5 is something that would be hard to accomplish with just a regular bar chart of the genres)

The radar chart also suggests that Efron is very balanced and well rounded meaning that between the generes hes acted in, the movies typically due fairly well (according to the average).
